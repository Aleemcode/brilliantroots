# brilliantroots Site Copy

Single source of truth for brilliantroots website copy.

## Copy Direction

brilliantroots is a product-led Islamic family brand with three connected layers:

- **Products:** physical tools and family learning materials for Muslim homes.
- **Tools:** digital/support tools that aid parenting upon correct manhaj and tarbiyah.
- **Store:** curated Muslim intellectual work, including paid works, free guides, parental kits, and author-led resources.

Primary audience:

- Muslim parents who want beneficial tools and guidance for the home.
- Prospective spouses preparing for family life with clarity.
- Muslim families who already have some foundation and want to consolidate, grow in eeman, and nurture righteous roots.

Primary conversion goal:

- Help visitors discover the right product, then buy, preorder, or join the waitlist based on product availability.

Voice:

- Clear, warm, grounded, and purposeful.
- Confident without hype.
- Benefit-led, not feature-heavy.
- No fabricated claims, statistics, or testimonials.

## Product Availability CTA System

Product availability must be controlled by backend/product data.

Status values:

- `1 = Available to buy`
- `2 = Preorder or waitlist`
- `3 = Coming soon`

CTA copy by status:

| Status | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA | Microcopy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `1` | Buy Now | View Product Details | Available now while stock lasts. |
| `2` | Preorder Now | Join the Waitlist | Reserve yours or get notified when the next batch opens. |
| `3` | Join the Waitlist | Get Launch Updates | This product is being prepared. Join the list for updates. |

Product card CTA variants:

- `1`: Shop Now
- `2`: Preorder
- `3`: Coming Soon

Product detail CTA variants:

- `1`: Buy [Product Name]
- `2`: Preorder [Product Name]
- `3`: Get [Product Name] Updates

Annotation:

- The copy avoids hardcoding availability. The UI should render CTA labels from status-aware copy so each product can move from coming soon to preorder to live purchase without rewriting the page.

# Copy Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before implementing or publishing copy.

- Each page has one primary job.
- Headlines are clear before they are creative.
- Product descriptions explain benefit, not only contents.
- No fake testimonials, fake statistics, or unsupported claims.
- Product CTA copy responds to backend availability status.
- Store copy includes paid and free works.
- Pathway copy speaks to prospective spouses and families.
- Tools remain separate from physical products.
- Baytul Asmaa appears under Tools, not Products.
- Placeholder published works are clearly marked for replacement.
